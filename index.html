How it works

Put your plans in motion

Built for community organizations, event teams, and nonprofits to manage events, positions, and volunteers in one place.

📋

Define volunteer positions

Create roles with schedules, capacity limits, and required skills. See at a glance how full each position is.

👤

Roster your volunteers

Add volunteers with their availability, skills, and contact info. Keep everyone's details in one place.

✋

Drag to assign

Simply drag a volunteer card onto a position. Double-booking and over-capacity are prevented automatically.

📊

See the full picture

"View all" shows a bird's-eye overview of every position and volunteer, with assignments clearly shown.

✨

Auto assign feature

Auto assign feature automatically matches volunteers to positions based on availability and skills. Makes coordination simple and quick.

🔒

Private

Volunteer contact info never leaves your account. You're in full control of your community's data.
