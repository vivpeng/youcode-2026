How it works

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Built for community organizers, event teams, and nonprofits who just need to get people in the right places — without a learning curve.

📋

Define your positions

Create roles with schedules, capacity limits, and required skills. See at a glance how full each position is.

👤

Roster your volunteers

Add volunteers with their availability, skills, and contact info. Keep everyone's details in one place.

✋

Drag to assign

Simply drag a volunteer card onto a position. The app prevents double-booking and over-capacity automatically.

📊

See the full picture

Switch to "View all" for a bird's-eye overview of every position and volunteer, with assignments clearly shown.

💾

Saves automatically

Your data is stored right in your browser. No accounts, no servers, no subscriptions. Just close the tab and come back.

🔒

Private by default

Volunteer contact info never leaves your device. You're in full control of your community's data.
